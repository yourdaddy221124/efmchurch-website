OUR MISSION

A Legacy of Faith in Andheri

Our church is a vibrant English-speaking congregation located just 3 kms from the Mumbai airport. We are part of the Free Methodist tradition, emphasizing biblical holiness, social justice, and personal transformation through Jesus Christ.

Whether you are a traveler visiting Mumbai or a resident looking for a spiritual home, our doors are open. We gather every Sunday for meaningful worship, sound biblical teaching, and genuine fellowship.
